Frequently Asked Questions

You're all thinking about them!

What is PixieHacks()?

PixieHacks is a 12 hour female-centric hackathon for high school students that has a strong focus on female immersion, empowerment, and exposure in the tech industry. It's meant to encourage more women to join the tech industry, by introducing them to like-minded females, providing them with both technical and non-technical workshops and presentations by strong female role models, and potentially giving away company tours, swag, and other opportunities as prizes.

What exactly *is* a hackathon?

A hackathon is an event, typically lasting several hours (or even days), where people meet to code, learn, eat, and show off what they've made in that time. You can find a longer explanation here!

What happens if I can't code?

You learn! We are open to hackers of all coding levels, whether you've don't don't know what a variable is or you build apps in your sleep. We will provide mentors and workshops so that everyone can complete a project that they're proud of.

What do I need to bring, in terms of equipment and money?

You should bring your own computer and charger, some form of identification, excitement :D, and power strips (recommended). A few loaner laptops may be available, but don't rely on that. This event costs nothing--each hacker will be provided with food, swag (even more than what you have already!), and FUNFUNFUN.

Who can attend?

Any female student still in high school is welcome to attend! However, if you are a male or not in high school, you're more than welcome to be a mentor! Email us at thepixiehacks@gmail.com if you have a situation you wish to explain to us :)

How do the teams work?

We recommend working in a team, but you have the option to work as an individual. The only limitation is that you can have no more than four people. Try to work with someone new! We'll have team building activities to assist with this.

I know a company that wants to sponsor, what should I tell them?

That's swagulous! Tell them to contact us at thepixiehacks@gmail.com and we'll take it from there.

Can I be a mentor at def hacks()?

Of course! The more help the better! Just fill out this typeform and we will contact you from there.
